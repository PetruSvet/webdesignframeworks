Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lif? + −

Lif is a mental health app that provides digital therapies, exercises, and personalized guidance to help you manage stress, anxiety, and other challenges.

How does Lif work? + −

Lif uses interactive exercises, guided meditations, and AI-driven chat support to help you identify emotions, reduce stress, and improve mental wellbeing at your own pace.

Is my data safe? + −

Yes, Lif uses end-to-end encryption and adheres to strict privacy policies to ensure your personal information and chat data remain secure.

Do I need a therapist? + −

Lif is designed for self-guided support, but it can complement professional therapy. You can use Lif on your own, and consult a therapist if you need additional guidance.

Can I use Lif for free? + −

Yes, Lif offers a free version with access to core exercises and AI chat. Premium features like advanced programs and personalized plans are available via subscription.

Which devices support Lif? + −

Lif is available on iOS, Android, and web browsers, so you can access it anywhere.

How often should I use Lif? + −

We recommend using Lif daily for best results, even if it's just 5–10 minutes for a quick exercise or check-in.

Can I track my progress? + −

Yes, Lif tracks your completed exercises, meditation minutes, and progress over time to help you stay motivated.
